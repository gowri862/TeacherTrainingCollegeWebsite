Details About Courses

Diploma In Nursery

Diploma in Nursery Teacher Training, popularly known as NTT is a course that primarily deals with the growth and learning of young minds. It is one of the most sought-after courses to become a pre- school teacher. The main aspect of the course is to facilitate better teaching practices and improving the quality of education at the nursery level. Aspiring Nursery teachers are trained in areas such as teaching methods, childcare and health, child psychology, communication and more. Being sensitive to children and their requirements is just one of the prerequisites to become a Nursery Teacher apart from having a personality that's pleasing enough to a 2.5 to 3.5-year-old child. Diploma in NTT is a one-year programme that gives a thorough and detailed understanding of the job role and trains them well to give children the best possible start to education.
